# Biographies of Speakers

Below, a brief biography of the speakers can be found:

[**Lavinia Stiliadou**](https://www.iaas.uni-stuttgart.de/institut/team/Stiliadou-00001/) research associate and PhD candidate at the Institute of Architecture of Application Systems (IAAS) at the University of Stuttgart, Germany.
She received her Master of Science (M.Sc.) degree in Computer Science from the University of Stuttgart in 2023 and has since transitioned into full-time academic research. 

#### Research Focus & Interests
Her primary research interests lie at the intersection of **Quantum Software Engineering** and cloud computing architectures. Specifically, her work focuses on:
* **Accessibility in Quantum Computing:** Developing software abstractions that lower the entry barrier for classical developers and domain experts.
* **Low-Code/No-Code Platforms:** Designing visual modeling environments that abstract complex quantum gate-level circuits into high-level domain workflows.
* **Hybrid Cloud Orchestration:** Managing unified and automated execution layers across heterogeneous quantum hardware and cloud middleware systems.
